Five Practices from Barbara Oakley

Two kinds of attention

Deep knowledge arises when you alternate between focused and absent-minded thinking.

Recall

Remember what you learned - this will allow you to connect disparate chunks of memory

Interleaving

Learn several skills at the same time, they enrich each other

Questions

While listening to the teacher, come up with a good question that really worries you.

The illusion of competence

Repeat to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: it only seems to you that you own a new topic

The story of Barbara Oakley

Since childhood, Barbara has not mathematics was given. She considered herself a complete humanitarian, and far from the smartest. In the army, she studied Russian to receive a bonus, so successfully that she was promoted to the commander. But for promotions had to pass math. And then Barbara I came up with my own approach to the exact sciences. It turned out if you got something "badly given", your hard-won knowledge is much deeper than those for whom everything is clear at a glance.
